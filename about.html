About Us

Here at NFT 214, giving chocolates for Valentine’s day just isn’t our style. Our team is focused on creating a Valentine’s NFT marketplace with endless masterpieces to choose from. To accomplish this, we have assembled the greatest minds in art, technology, web design and finance.

Our Mission Statement

From the beginning, our mission has been to provide a different type of valentines gift. Traditionally, people around us resort to giving things like chocolates, movie tickets, and clothes. What if you could show your appreciation for someone with a unique piece of collectible online art, that can grow in value? We consider it a privelage to be able to provide a wide array of digital art and allow for people to present their loved ones with a "non fungible" token of appreciation.

Our Personal Values

We as the NFT 214 Development Community strive to maintain a positive and inclusive experience for everyone. NFT 214 is committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all participants and cosumers, and expects everyone to treat each other with respect and courtesy. Our commitment to the highest standards helps us do commerce with great people, build great products, and attract loyal users. Respect for our users, for the opportunity, and for each other are foundational to our success and are something we need to support every day.
